## Domain / Module Selected

**Field Device** (priority 9) — physical sensor or actuator managed by an SPS Controller.

---

## Why This Candidate

Field Device is the direct child of SPS Controller in the infrastructure hierarchy and the natural next step. It also acts as the parent of BACnet Object (next domain). Establishing Field Device early enables BACnet Object to follow the same cross-module validation pattern.

---

## Dependency Classification

- Business logic: **In-process**.
- Persistence: **Local-substitutable** (SQLAlchemy + in-memory adapters).
- Cross-module dependency on SPS Controller: **In-process** (validates parent existence on create).

---

## Seam Decision

No new Seam introduced. `FieldDeviceRepository` follows the same Protocol pattern used throughout. One real Seam exists: `SqlAlchemyFieldDeviceAdapter` (production) + `InMemoryFieldDeviceAdapter` (tests).

---

## Interface Chosen

**Common Caller Interface** — 5 entry points mirroring SPS Controller:

- `create_device(command)` → `Result[FieldDevice, ...]`
- `get_device(query)` → `Result[FieldDevice, FieldDeviceNotFoundError]`
- `list_devices(query)` → `Page[FieldDevice]`
- `update_device(command)` → `Result[FieldDevice, ...]`
- `delete_device(controller_id, device_id)` → `Result[None, FieldDeviceNotFoundError]`

The Interface enforces that names are stripped and validated via `FieldDeviceName.parse()`, that duplicate names under the same controller are rejected, and that the parent SPS Controller must exist before creation.
